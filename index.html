📖 How to Play

The Goal

Fill in each circle so that each row, column, and chain contains each number exactly once. For example, in a 4×4 puzzle each row, column, and chain will contain the numbers 1–4 exactly once.

What is a Chain?

Conflicts are highlighted automatically in red as you play — no need to check manually.

Challenging difficulty leaves only the absolute minimum clues (~N given numbers). These puzzles may require advanced logic and are guaranteed to have a unique solution.

Chains are groups of circles connected by lines. Each chain snakes across the board and must also contain each number exactly once — just like rows and columns.

Given Numbers

The yellow circles are clues given to you at the start. They cannot be changed.
